Popular Destinations in India

  • Goa – Famous for its beaches, nightlife, and Portuguese heritage.
  • Kerala – Known for backwaters, hill stations, and Ayurveda retreats.
  • Rajasthan – Explore forts, palaces, and the Thar Desert.
  • Delhi – India’s vibrant capital with historical monuments.
  • Agra – Home to the iconic Taj Mahal.
  • Jaipur – The Pink City with royal charm and culture.
  • Leh-Ladakh – Breathtaking landscapes and mountain passes.
  • Andaman Islands – Pristine beaches and scuba diving paradise.
